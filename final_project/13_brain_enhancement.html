In the very near future we are looking at enhancement of the brain via nootropics (chemicals that improve brain function) and through prosthetic extension ( brain computer interfaces, implants, brain-wave reading devices). This is an exciting topic and a hotly debated topic as society tries to come to grips with the ethics, impact to society, culture, etc.
