Robots have been with us for many years now (dishwashers, auto-pilots, etc). However, we are entering a new realm of robotic intelligence. Estimates vary but shortly we will have robotics with very advanced intelligence which will be able to do a variety of tasks. Domestic robots will help the aging population with meal preparation, taking readings which will be relayed to medical facilities, etc. Robots will impact all areas of human activity.
