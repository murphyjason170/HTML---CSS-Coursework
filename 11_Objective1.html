Southern Culture On The Skids

I chose a partial listing of the discography of the band "Southern Culture on The Skids". They have a rockabilly/surf sound that always makes me laugh with their fun zany lyrics.

I arranged the 1990s discography by record label. I also put web links in the table which was fun. Working through this I learned that I prefer to put each column on its own line for improved readability of the html code.

I chose to use columns to delineate the data categories since it looks clean!
